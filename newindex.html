Scratch Cooking

Everything we plate is prepared from scratch, in our kitchen. Even our calamari are cut from whole squid, breaded with our own recipe, and fried to order. We work very hard to avoid both ordinary, and pre-packaged food items. Enjoy!

Prime Meats

Less than 2% of all beef in the U.S. is certified USDA Prime. This tender, young and well marbled beef seldom reaches stores, as it is allocated to the very best steak houses across the country. Butcher & Barrel is one of those steak houses.

Fresh, Never Frozen

We would save a lot of money if we purchased product in bulk and stored it in freezers for months until you come in to order, but that wouldn’t taste as good. So we don’t. Occasionally, this means we might run out of a product or two. Now you know why.

Seasonal Ingredients

We embrace each new season, featuring the fresh produce that becomes available, and then craft unique recipes that highlight these seasonal ingredients. Yeah, we know that you might be a creature of habit, but we like to change things up a few times a year.

Food Menu

Craft Cocktails

We give the same attention to our alcohol options as we do our food.
After all, what is good food without good drink? The major differences are that we focus
on premium spirits, quality ingredients, and fresh juices. We don’t carry the typical bottles
of pre-made mixers. After all, we don’t want to offend or pollute a good quality alcohol.

Drunken Cherries

Maraschino cherries are an abomination – produced by first stripping
them of all color using sulfur dioxide and calcium chloride - then re-coloring them with red dye #40.
We don’t serve them. We use only dark Morello cherries. Storing them in a jar of bourbon gives
them a little extra “kick”! Sober cherries available upon request.

Cold as Ice

While all ice is indeed just “frozen water”, how it behaves
in your drink depends a whole lot on its size and shape. A small nugget quickly chills
a refreshing cold drink – but melts quickly (drink fast!). A large ice cube is better for a
premium spirit – when you want to sip slowly, and not have your drink watered down.

Desserts & Coffee

What is a fine meal without the proper finish?
We make all of our desserts from scratch, in house. They’re delicious. We also offer
French Press coffee, featuring beans from Wilson’s coffee in Racine. And we have a small selection
of fine ports and liqueurs for those that would enjoy a nightcap.
